Installing the tools

This describe how to install the tools for importing eRA in the different databases
Because none of us have the era drive mapped at the same letter, you need to copy the version which is in the era Drive into your local D:era directory

On your D: drive

First you might want to back up or rename the previous version of the era tool. Right click on the D:\era folder and select Rename.

From the Cardamom e-RA drive:

When you are aware that there is a new version of the era tool: select the folder, right click and Copy.
Then paste it in your D: drive : or any other method you know to copy this file.
